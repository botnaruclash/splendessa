The roadmap

A clear path from first call to fully booked.

No vague timelines, no agency mystery. This is the exact process every Splendessa project follows — so you know what happens, when, and what you get at each step.

01
Day 1

Diagnostic & strategy call

A free 30-minute call where we audit your current website and lead flow. We identify exactly where enquiries are leaking — slow load, unclear booking path, no follow-up — and quantify what it's costing you.

  • Full audit of your current site & booking flow
  • Revenue leak estimate specific to your clinic
  • A clear recommendation — even if it's not us
02
Days 2–5

Design & conversion copy

We design your new website around your brand and your highest-value treatments. Every headline, section, and button is written to move a visitor toward one action: booking a consultation.

  • Custom design — never a template
  • Conversion copywriting with a clear offer
  • One structured revision round — fast and focused
03
Days 5–10

Build, integrate & automate

Development with obsessive attention to speed and mobile experience. We connect your booking software and install the lead-response automations that recover enquiries your front desk can't reach in time.

  • Booking integration (Zenoti, Boulevard, Mindbody, Vagaro)
  • Missed-call text-back & instant lead alerts
  • Local SEO foundation & sub-second load speed
04
Days 10–14

Launch, measure & grow

Your new system goes live. We monitor the first leads coming through, fine-tune the automations, and stay close with post-launch support so the system keeps converting month after month.

  • Zero-downtime launch on your domain
  • Database reactivation campaign for past clients
  • Ongoing support & optimization
